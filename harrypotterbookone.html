Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone:
a story mapping series

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone is the first book in the critically-acclaimed and best-selling series by J.K. Rowling.
It is in this first book that we are not only introduced to our protagonist eleven year-old Harry Potter, but also
to a colorful cast of characters, a magical world full of wonder, and a dangerous mystery to solve.

In the following sections of this book mapping series, we will take a quick look at the cast of Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone,
followed by a look into the world and its fantastical elements, and finally how secrets and mystery drive the story forward.
